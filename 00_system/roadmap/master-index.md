## Mastery Bar (applies to every concept, every track)

1. Reproduce from blank file, from memory, no lookups
2. Explain each line / decision out loud
3. Three varied versions solving different problems

Mark `status: mastered` in Obsidian frontmatter when all three pass. Re-test weekly via spaced repetition.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Never break the streak — a 20-min day counts, a skipped day does not
2. Mastery bar applies to every concept without exception
3. Sunday afternoon off — recovery is part of the work
4. Weekly spaced re-tests — this is what makes learning permanent
5. Public writing starts month 4 — one essay every two weeks minimum
6. Daily Obsidian log — 90 seconds, every day
